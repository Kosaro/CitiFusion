Welcome to CitiFusion! We make sure to provide assistance and incentives to follow ESG practices. Join us, and learn more about it in our About Us page!









































CitiFusion provides a multitude of benefits for all companies and individuals involved; we bring together those with the common goal of a sustainable and ethical future for our planet and all who inhabit it.

    Vendors will receive:

  • Support and guidance in following ESG practices
  • A Citi ESG Certification
  • The opportunity to work with like-minded businesses
  • Enchanced market outreach

    Small businesses will receive:

  • Lower and transparent prices from suppliers
  • Additional 1% cashback on all purchases through the CitiFusion platform with a Citi credit card
  • Resources and expertise to adapt their business model
  • Marketing their sustainable practices to consumers concerned with issues related to ESG
